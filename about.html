Welcome to MeCollage

MeCollage is a free online image collage maker designed to help you create beautiful photo collages quickly and easily. Our mission is to provide a simple, powerful, and privacy-focused tool that anyone can use to transform their photos into stunning visual stories.

Whether you're preserving family memories, creating social media content, or designing a portfolio, MeCollage gives you the tools to create professional-looking collages without the complexity of expensive software.

Our Story

We launched MeCollage in 2025 with a simple goal: to make photo collage creation accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone should be able to preserve and share their memories beautifully, without needing expensive software or technical expertise.

We noticed that many existing collage tools were either too complicated for casual users or lacked essential features. Some required downloads, while others had intrusive ads or privacy concerns. We set out to create a tool that addressed all these issues.

Today, MeCollage continues to evolve based on user feedback, with a focus on simplicity, performance, and privacy. We're committed to keeping our tool free and accessible to everyone.

What We Offer

20 Photos Per Collage

Create collages with up to 20 photos, perfect for showcasing multiple memories.

Multiple Aspect Ratios

Choose from 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, 3:4, and 16:9 ratios for any platform.

Customizable Design

Adjust spacing, rounded corners, and other design elements.

Photo Filters

Enhance your photos with various filters and effects.

Text & Stickers

Add text and fun stickers to personalize your collages.

Privacy Focused

All processing happens locally in your browser - no data sent to servers.

Our Values

Privacy First

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Simplicity

An easy-to-use interface that anyone can master in minutes, without sacrificing powerful features.

Quality

Professional-grade results without the professional price tag. We strive for excellence in every aspect of our tool.

Accessibility

Free for everyone, everywhere. No hidden fees, no watermarks, no signup required.

Built with Modern Technology

MeCollage is built using cutting-edge web technologies to ensure a fast, reliable, and secure experience:

  • Built with modern web technologies (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript)
  • No server-side photo processing - everything happens in your browser
  • Fast and responsive design
  • Works on all devices with a web browser
  • Regular updates to improve performance and add new features
