Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal size for Instagram Story collages?

Instagram Stories use a 9:16 aspect ratio (1080x1920 pixels). Our tool automatically creates collages in this perfect vertical format.

How many photos can I include in one story collage?

You can add up to 20 photos in a single collage. For best results on Stories, we recommend using 2-6 photos for clear visibility.

Can I add text to my Instagram Story collage?

Yes! You can add custom text to your collage, perfect for captions, quotes, or branding. Double-click the text on the canvas to edit.

Do my collages have watermarks?

No! All collages are exported without watermarks. You get clean, professional results every time.

What formats can I export my collage in?

You can export your Instagram Story collage in both PNG and JPG formats, whichever works best for your needs.
