Frequently Asked Questions

MeCollage is a free online image collage maker that allows you to create beautiful collages with unlimited images. It offers professional layout options, customization features, and support for up to 20 photos per collage.

Simply click or drag and drop your photos into the upload area. Once uploaded, your images will be automatically arranged into a collage. You can then customize the layout, add filters, text, or stickers to perfect your design.

You can add up to 20 images per collage. The tool supports various formats including PNG, JPG, and HEIC.

MeCollage supports multiple aspect ratios including 1:1, 4:5, 9:16, 3:4, and 16:9, allowing you to create collages optimized for different platforms and purposes.

Yes! You can adjust the gutter spacing between images, add rounded corners, apply various filters, insert text, and add emoji stickers to personalize your collage. The intuitive toolbar at the bottom of the canvas gives you quick access to all these features.

Once you're satisfied with your collage, you can export it using the export functionality. The tool uses html2canvas to generate a high-quality image of your collage for download.

Yes, MeCollage is completely free to use. You can create unlimited collages without any watermarks, signup requirements, or hidden fees.
