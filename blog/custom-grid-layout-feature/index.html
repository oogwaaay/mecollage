Custom Grid Layout: Personalize Your Collage Arrangements

2026-01-28

We're excited to announce the new Custom Grid Layout feature in MeCollage! While our auto-layout is great for quick creations, sometimes you need more control. Now, you can specify exactly how many rows and columns your collage should have.

How to Use Custom Grid Layout

  • 1. Upload your photos (up to 20).
  • 2. In the Sidebar, look for the 'Grid Layout' section.
  • 3. Toggle from 'Auto' to 'Custom'.
  • 4. Enter your desired Rows and Columns (e.g., 2x3, 4x5).
  • 5. Click 'Apply' to see your new layout instantly!

Why Use Custom Grids?

  • Precise Control: Match your layout to the exact number of photos you have.
  • Creative Freedom: Experiment with unconventional grids like 1x5 or 5x1.
  • Professional Look: Ensure all photos are the same size and perfectly aligned.
