Step-by-Step Tutorial

1

Choose Your Aspect Ratio

Select the format that fits your needs:

  • 1:1 Square - Perfect for Instagram posts and profile pictures
  • 9:16 Vertical - Ideal for Instagram Stories, TikTok, and mobile wallpapers
  • 16:9 Landscape - Great for YouTube banners and presentations
  • 4:5 Portrait - Optimized for Instagram vertical posts
2

Upload Your Photos

Add up to 20 photos from your device. Simply:

  • Click the upload area to browse files
  • Or drag and drop photos directly onto the page
  • Supported formats: PNG, JPG, HEIC
  • No file size limits
3

Arrange Your Layout

Your photos will be automatically arranged, but you can customize:

  • Gutter Spacing - Adjust space between photos (0-50px)
  • Corner Radius - Add rounded corners for a softer look
  • Photo Order - Drag to rearrange images
4

Add Creative Touches

Personalize your collage with:

  • Photo Filters - Noir, Retro, Fade, Glow, Cool effects
